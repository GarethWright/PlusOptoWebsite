TFT Display Solutions from Winstar for Pro Audio Front Panels

21 April 2026

Designing a front panel for pro audio equipment involves more than selecting a display that fits the available space. In products such as DSP units, digital mixers, power amplifiers, and controllers, display choice also affects interface clarity, mechanical layout, and overall integration flexibility.

This featured introduction looks at TFT display solutions for pro audio front panels across 1U to 4U rack-mounted systems. It covers compact and bar-type display formats, along with practical considerations such as readability, structural fit, optical treatment, interface support, and scalable development concepts.

For teams developing professional audio equipment, this provides a useful overview of how different TFT display options can support front-panel design across a range of product configurations.
